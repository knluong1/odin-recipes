Brendon's lasagna

Description

Best lasagna from yo mama

Ingredients

  • 16 ounce lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 jar spagetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 pound shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until aldente; drain
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown beef and season with salt and pepper; drain. Stir in spagetti sauce and garlic and simmer 5 mintues.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, Cheddar and ricotta; stir well. In 9x13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese mixture until pan is lifted.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly
